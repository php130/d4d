GNSS Dataset Under Jamming, Spoofing, and Meaconing Conditions

Authors/Creators

  • ortiz, miguel1

Description

This dataset contains GNSS signal recordings collected under controlled attack scenarios—including jamming, spoofing, meaconing, and combined attacks—performed at various power levels and frequency bands during Jammertest 2024. Data were captured from both stationary and dynamic receiver conditions, including raw UBX files, RINEX observations, RF monitoring, and navigation solutions. The structured dataset enables analysis and development of detection and mitigation techniques against GNSS interference.
